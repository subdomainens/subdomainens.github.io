Buy ENS Subdomain


Navigate to the 'Name Information Tab', enter the name of interest and check whether it is available for purchase. If so, the value 'True' will appear next to 'For Sale'.

To then buy an ENS subdomain, navigate to the 'Buy Name' tab, enter your address, enter the full ens name (e.g. mysubdomain.ensname.eth), enter the name's price (found in the 'Name Information' tab), then click 'Buy'.



Sell ENS Subdomain


Our Dapp acts as a temporary custodian of the deed of your ENS name and its owner for a "commit" period which you must specify (must be longer than 7 days). Once that period has passed you may reclaim ownership of your deed and ENS name.



To sell subdomains on your ENS name, navigate to the 'Sell Name' tab, enter your ETH address, enter the "commit" period (in days), the expiry of subdomains (must be less than or equal to the commit period), enter the price click 'List' then 'Finalize'.
